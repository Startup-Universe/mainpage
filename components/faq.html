Frequently Asked Questions.

Who can participate in an SU program?

In SU programs can participate everyone from individuals to part of a team/company with a tech idea/product that can make an impact and scale globally. The SU programs are suitable for early-stage tech startups on the idea, pre-seed and seed round and open for every founder.

What is the cost to participate in an SU program?

There is no cost for the participating founders. The only thing required is your time and effort to take advantage of our programs to their fullest extent. Programs run completely free.

What is the duration of the national programs, and where will they be hosted?

All the national programs have a duration of 4 weeks, starting on March 2023. The programs will be hosted 100% at the Founderhood's platform.

Is it possible for late-stage startups (growth-expansion-exit) to participate in the program?

Yes, the Startup Universe is a 100% open initiative that tech startups on every stage could join - there is no selection process. We have seen that every startup has different needs, and we attempted to make our programs fully flexible so that startups can benefit from them.

How many founders/tech startups can participate from each country? Is there a selection process?

There is no limit to the number of participants in the national programs and no selection process for the participants who want to join. Since one of our main goals is to democratize innovation and access to resources for every founder, we organized the Startup Universe as a 100% online program open to everyone.

What if I am interested in joining a country’s program, but I am come from/am based on a different country?

If you can't find your country between the participating nations or you just want to participate on another national program you can register for any other national program. However keep in mind that most national programs take place using the native language.
