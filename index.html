What is European Startup Universe?

European Startup Universe is the biggest project for EU tech startups, in collaboration with Founderhood, connecting thousands of early-stage startups with the best minds of the EU tech community - unicorn founders, known investors, and experts.

Our ambitious goal

Our goal is to support and connect 10.000 early-stage startups across the EU - run by EU founders from around the globe - with the brightest minds of the EU diaspora, late-stage founders, angel investors, corporates, and experts through massive 6-week online national programs open for every EU founder. We want to give access to opportunities for funding, expansion across EU countries, and opportunities to find new clients to grow revenues.

How is ESU going to achieve this?

By bringing together European countries to join forces and launch 6-week online national programs. Every participating EU country will launch a program suitable for their nation. All 6-week programs will take place simultaneously on the Founderhood platform, in March-April 2022.

Who supports this whole initiative?

The non-profit initiative of the European Startup Universe is under the patronage of the European Committee of the Regions, and Mariya Gabriel, European Commissioner for Innovation, Research, Culture, Education, and Youth.

Frequently Asked Questions.

Who can participate in an ESU program?

Everyone who has an EU nationality or lives in an EU country, and is either individual or part of a team/company with a tech idea/product that can make an impact and scale globally. The ESU programs are suitable for early-stage tech startups on the idea, pre-seed and seed round and open for every EU founder.

What is the cost to participate in an ESU program?

There is no cost for the participating founders. The only thing required is your time and effort to take advantage of our programs to their fullest extent.

What is the duration of the national programs, and where will they be hosted?

All the national programs have a duration of 6 weeks, starting from March 2022 until early April 2022. The programs will be hosted 100% at the Founderhood's platform.

Is it possible for late-stage startups (growth-expansion-exit) to participate in the program?

Yes, the European Startup Universe is a 100% open program that tech startups on every stage could join - there is no selection process. We have seen that every startup has different needs, and we attempted to make our programs fully flexible so that startups can benefit from them.

What if I am an EU founder but cannot find my country's program to attend?

Eleven ESU Country Organizers have joined forces to organize a European Startup Universe program in their country. If you can't find your country between them, you can register for any other national program.

How many founders/tech startups can participate from each country? Is there a selection process?

There is no limit to the number of participants in the national programs and no selection process for the participants who want to join. Since one of our main goals is to democratize access for every EU founder, we organized the European Startup Universe as a 100% online program open to everyone.

What if I am interested in joining, but I am not from the EU?

As we want to make Europe the new global powerhouse for startups, the European Startup Universe is designed to be more valuable for people related to the EU tech ecosystem (located in or originated from an EU country) than those with nothing to do with it. We aspire for our programs to be implemented worldwide in the future so every tech founder in the world can participate and benefit from themt. Stay tuned!
